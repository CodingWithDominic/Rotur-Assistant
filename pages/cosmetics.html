As of now, cosmetics can only be directly uploaded by Mistium herself. This is likely done to ensure cosmetic quality stays at least acceptable, and the price of a given cosmetic at least somewhat matches its quality.

While Rotur Assistant doesn't provide the tools to submit or upload a cosmetic, it will at least provide a guide and a starting point on creating one.

Right now, Rotur Assistant only provides the tools for creating avatar overlay cosmetics. Rotur Assistant plans to support more types once they are added.

Below is an overlay template to get you started on creating an overlay. You will need some form of image editing software that (ideally and preferrably) supports transparency. The software recommend by the developer of Rotur Assistant is paint.net (and also ezgif if you're making an animated cosmetic). The avatar area within the template has a diameter of 240px (contrary to Rotur avatars being 256x256), while the template itself is 288x288. The scale factor of the overlay compared to the PFP is 1.2x appearance-wise, and 1.125x size-wise.
