Rotur Services

Rotur has many official services. Examples include RoturPhotos, RoturGate, and originChats. Below is the list of all known Rotur services.

Rotur Photos

Rotur's equivalent to Google Photos. However, since Rotur Accounts only have 10 MB of RoturPhotos storage (more if you upgrade), this is not really a meta place to store photos in the cloud. To Rotur's credit though, unlike companies like Google or Microsoft, the creator of Rotur doesn't have the budget or resources to easily provide gigabytes of free storage to its users.

Rotur Notes

Rotur's equivalent to a cloud notes app. Can be useful if you carry your workflow across devices, such as school-issued devices.

Rotur Music

Rotur's equivalent to Spotify. However, to avoid having to worry about licensing or copyright, the people who can actually use Rotur Music is hand-picked, though people can host their own versions of Rotur Music with their own music libraries if they want.

Rotur Git

Rotur's equivalent to GitHub, based on Forgejo. After this service was created, Mistium moved a good amount of Rotur and OriginChats's repos over to there from GitHub. If you want to use GitHub and RoturGit at the same time, there's an option to have a GitHub mirror repo on RoturGit, which syncs GitHub changes in real-time to the RoturGit repo. Rotur Assistant has a repo on there (though it's just a mirror of the Github repo).

Rotur Gate

Rotur's URL shortener. Free accounts are limited to 50 URLs, each of them being randomly generated, while accounts with a high enough subscription can have more URLs, alongside the ability to customize them.

Rotur Gifts

Create Rotur gifts, send them in a public OriginChats chat, and watch the chaos ensue. This service was mainly created to complement OriginChats gifting, inspired by a client developer incorporating gifting into their OriginChats client. While there is no official portal to manage the gifts you created, there is an unused API for it. Therefore, Rotur Assistant provides a portal for gift management.

Devfund

Rotur's crowdfunding website to promote things like apps, services, functionality to existing services, or external products. People can make petitions and approve developers for them, but only Mistium has the power to mark petitions as complete, rewarding the developer with any accumulated funds. As of now, there is an estimated total of around 1,184.39 Rotur Credits up for grabs in unclaimed devfund petitions.

Appsie

This is basically a more centralized developer portal for people making apps for originOS.

Rmail

Rotur's version of e-mail, allowing for private, one-on-one conversations between 2 users. Not many clients exist for it, but thankfully, Rotur Assistant provides one. Today, OriginChats DMs is preferred for one-on-one communication since it's more real-time than e-mail.

Claw

Claw is Rotur's version of a twitter-like website. This was basically Rotur's primary social media service, before the existence of OriginChats. Various clients exist for it, including the one provided by Rotur Assistant.

OriginChats

The Rotur Ecosystem's replacement for Discord. It offers more freedom and flexibility than Discord, allowing you to host a server on your own hardware (or have someone do it for you if you don't have such hardware). It is also far more lightweight than Discord. OriginChats was created around 9 months before the people of Rotur decided to switch to OriginChats as their primary way of communicating with each other. The versatility of OriginChats also allows people to create clients for it too, though the recommended one is the official one.
