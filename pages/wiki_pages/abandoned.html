Abandoned Features or Services

Origin-related or Rotur-related features that were worked on briefly, but then abandoned and likely forgotten about by Mistium. Features that are classified as finished won't be listed here.

Rotur Groups

A Rotur feature that presumably allowed for Rotur users to form public or private groups, represent them, communicate with one-another in them, etc. It was worked on for around a few days, before being effectively forgotten about since then.

Rotur Search

This was mainly created because Mistium was bored one time and wanted an excuse to create another repo under roturTW. Rotur assistant offers its own version of Rotur Search, in the form of Lookup.

Rotur Beam

This was meant to be Rotur's equivalent to Apple's AirDrop feature, which allows you to quickly send and receive files to and from other users.
