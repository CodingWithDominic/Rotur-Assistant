Claw

Rotur's first social media

Overview

Claw was Rotur's first real attempt at a social media platform. It has a style/layout inspired by that of Twitter. Users can share posts up to 300 characters long, or 600 characters if they have claw premium. Users can also repost other users' claw posts. Claw also has image support, though the image needs to be externally hosted. The claw posts a user make are stored in their profile data, unless "include_posts=no" is added to the URL query parameter.

Clients

People can also make clients for claw. Claw even has a tag clients can use when a post is sent to include in that post the client the claw post was created on (though the client has to be registered under https://api.rotur.dev/systems first). Rotur Assistant also provides a claw client, with some unique properties: Real-time feed support, no need to host images yourself thanks to the client handling that for you, and lastly, the ability to choose which system you appear from.
