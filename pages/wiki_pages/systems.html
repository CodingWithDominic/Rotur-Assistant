Rotur Systems

Overtime, people have made systems for Rotur. Rotur Systems are usually web-OSes, Rotur utility tools (like this one!), or ecosystems that utilize the ease of cross-compatibility that Rotur accounts provide. However, a good chunk of these systems are either a WIP, abandoned, or are simply placeholder. Below is a list of all the systems that can be found at https://api.rotur.dev/systems:

originOS

Main article: originOS
Owner: Mist | Designation: ori | Link

The most well-known system out of all the Rotur systems. OriginOS is a scratch-based web operating system that aims to provide users with an interface for cross-device cloud work. OriginOS is the primary reason Rotur exists today, since Rotur started as an account manager for originOS. For a lot of newcomers to the greater Rotur community, originOS is the primary way of interacting with a lot of Rotur. OriginOS is also home to a programming language you may have seen be brought up every now and then: OSL.

rotur

Main article: Rotur
Owner: Mist | Designation: rtr | Link

Rotur is the backbone of all the other afformentioned systems. Accounts created through rotur.dev/auth that doesn't have a URL parameter specifying the system default to the accounts being under the "rotur" system.

Rotur Assistant

Main article: About
Owner: Dominic | Designation: ast | Link

Rotur Assistant is the 'system' you're on right now! Well, I personally don't classify it as a system; it's moreso a Rotur utility tool. I registered Rotur Assistant as a "system" so that any options that prompt you to select your system (such as making a claw post) will have a default option. Not only that, it serves as a nice mode of recognition too, especially with the badge I designed for it.

Asier System

Owner: Asier Diamond | Designation: asi | Link

Asier System is a scratch-based web operating system, similar to originOS. Asier System comes with its own programming language too: ASPL. As of the time this is being written, Asier system recently released! When I mentioned Rotur Assistant to Asier Diamond, I made an agreement to not release Rotur Assistant near Asier System's release date (Rotur Assistant was delayed at the time anyways due to me needing to fix more bugs as well as more thorough testing from testers).

Constellinux

Owner: ThatBeaverDev | Designation: crl | Link

Constellinux was an operating system that was meant to be a Rotur-based, Linux-inspired scratch OS. While it did get decently far in development, it unfortunately got scrapped, or rather rebranded to Constellation. Today, while the creator is on-off in the Rotur community every now and then, mentions of either Constellinux or Constellation are a rarity.

HuopaOS

Owner: Allucat1000 | Designation: hup | Link

HuopaOS was an HTML-based web operating system. Unfortunately, it was abandoned in favor of PrismOS, though today, PrismOS seems to have been forgotten, alongside HuopaOS. Today, the developer has since shifted their OS developing efforts to assisting with ArcOS, as well as other personal side projects every now and then.

Nex

Owner: Milodev123 | Designation: nex

Nex is a WIP operating system, which was the successor of AsterOS. While Nex itself is still a massive WIP, it has spinoff products, such as NexPay, which was incorporated into the creator's own originChats client. NexPay would eventually go on to inspire Mistium to add a Rotur Gifts system.

OliveOS

Owner: Justnoone | Designation: ove

OliveOS is a WIP Linux (ubuntu)-based operating system. Development started on it sometime in the latter half of 2024.

PassNet

Owner: furina | Designation: pas

Unlike all other systems here, PassNet is not a real system. PassNet replaced explodeOS in https://api.rotur.dev/systems as a placeholder system, added there for fun (at the time, Mistium was really close friends with the person behind explodeOS / PassNet). Today, PassNet exists in https://api.rotur.dev/systems mainly as part of an agreement between Mistium and its owner, after they... went their own ways.

Warpdrive

Owner: Jax | Designation: wdv | Link

Warpdrive is an upcoming gaming console, being developed by Jax and his team. Warpdrive plans to use Rotur accounts for its account system.

flf

Owner: Flufi | Designation: flf

Flufi OS was an operating system developed by Flufi. Today, while all traces of Flufi OS have been removed, its designation in https://api.rotur.dev/systems still exists, under "flf".

geec os

Owner: geec | Designation: gec

Geec os was an OS developed by geec (a.k.a. Bob). While development looked promising, development presumably slowed down around mid to late 2025. Today, the creator is rarely seen in the Rotur community today.

kyrOS

Owner: NubbinsRuz (Main account: Neork) | Designation: kyr

KyrOS is a WIP OS that is home to its own programming language: kyr. Kyr is a C++-based language that can process millions of instructions per second, something languages like OSL and even JavaScript can only dream of. Today, kyrOS itself is in a state of on-off development.

orion

Owner: darkdot | Designation: nva | Link

Orion is Rotur Assistant before Rotur Assistant. It has its own finance manager, claw client, and rmail client. Part of the idea for Rotur Assistant came from Orion. Orion used to have an originChats client too, but that has since been discontinued in favor of Orla, a TUI-based client for originChats.

Everything past this point are systems that used to be Rotur-based, but have since been removed from https://api.rotur.dev/systems or replaced by another system.

ArcOS

Owner: izkuipers | Designation: arc | Link

ArcOS is a Web OS developed by izkuipers, Allucat1000 (as mentioned earlier), and 3 other people. ArcOS planned to have Rotur functionality, even having their own designation for it, but at some point, izkuipers wanted to move ArcOS off of Rotur, to be its own thing. As a result, izkuipers had his Rotur account deleted, and his system removed from https://api.rotur.dev/systems. ArcOS is still in development today, being worked on by the same 5 people mentioned earlier.

ShimaOS

Owner: shima | Designation: shi

ShimaOS was an OS that was developed by Mistium, who at the time, decided to go undercover as Shima, as a social experiment, (similar to the story behind Dav's existence). Surprisingly, work on ShimaOS continued somewhat, even after Mistium revealed that she was Shima. Today though, ShimaOS is now a relic of the past, with Mistium today continuing to focus her OS-developing efforts on originOS.

NovaOS

Owner: darkdot | Designation: nva | Link

Used to be listed on https://api.rotur.dev/systems, but has since been replaced in there by orion, still brandishing the nva designation surprisingly. In actuality though, orion and NovaOS appear to operate independently of one another (aside from the similar URLs)
