Discord

Overview

Discord is a popular chat platform, allowing for real-time communication, either privately in DMs or group chats, or publicly in servers, some of which can have 1,000,000+ members. While Discord is largely ad-free, Discord makes money through other means, such as quest partnerships (basically ads but you get rewarded for it), or Discord Nitro.

OriginOS on Discord

Prior to the mass-migration to OriginChats, the greater Rotur community resided on Discord. Some parts of Rotur, such as roturBOT, relied on Discord too. Since the Rotur community started from originOS, the Discord server is branded after originOS, rather than Rotur. Rotur once had its own Discord server, but it was eventually deleted due to lack of activity. Shortly after, the originOS Discord server rebranded itself to "originOS and roturTW", also bringing over some of the unique channels from the deleted Rotur Discord server. However, all the channels brought over from the Rotur discord server would go on to either be archived or deleted, in favor of project threads. Eventually, the originOS Discord server dropped the "and roturTW" part in its name, and is now today called just simply "origin". The originChats origin server shares the same name and icon as the Discord server.

The mass-migration

For a while, people have made mild suggestions to "use originChats lol", until it was no longer a joke. The Rotur community gradually got tired of many of the decisions Discord has been making over the past few years; things like UI changes, paywalling stuff behind Nitro, and even potential age verification. So one user, Allucat1000, stepped up and pioneered a mass-movement over to originChats. Mistium then made an annoncement that she would have her main platform be originChats for the next week. At the end of that week, Mistium decided to stay on originChats permanently. Furthermore, she decided to close or delete a lot of her accounts on various places, such as Google. Today, the Rotur community largely resides on originChats. As a result of the mass-migration, a lot of the channels in the Discord server were archived. The origin Discord server is no longer as active as before.

roturBOT

This is about the Discord bot. For the originChats counterpart of this bot, see OriginChats (roturBOT).

RoturBOT is a Discord bot that allows you to interact with Rotur and manage your Rotur account from within Discord. RoturBOT allows you to look up users, manage account keys, follow/friend/block other users, and more. It basically was Rotur's integration into Discord. Today, RoturBOT on Discord is in a state of ill-maintenance, since Mistium herself decided to leave Discord in favor of OriginChats. It's still used today in the origin Discord server in order to have Discord messages and OriginChats messages synced across the Discord server and the OriginChats server.
