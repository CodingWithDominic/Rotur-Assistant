Sync Data

This will sync any Rotur Account data you have, including the tokens of all the Rotur accounts you have added above, to your Google Account. While this will save time having to re-add all your accounts on another instance of Rotur Assistant in another window or on another device, this will effectively put the security of all your Rotur Accounts at the mercy of the security of your Google Account.

Due to Google limitations on sync storage size, uploading to sync is unavailable if you have more than 20 accounts added.
